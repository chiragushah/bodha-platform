🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to Bodha™! You're about to enter a world where learning about sustainability is genuinely fun. Here's everything you need to get started in minutes.

🖥️

System Requirements

All Bodha™ games run entirely in your web browser — no downloads needed.

1
Use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
2
Minimum 1GB RAM — any device made after 2018 works
3
Stable internet connection recommended (1 Mbps+)
4
For best audio narration, use Chrome with sound enabled
🎯

Choosing the Right Game

Each game teaches different skills — here's a quick guide:

1
EcoQuest™ — Ages 7–12, environment & ecology
2
MindQuest™ — Ages 8–14, emotional wellness & resilience
3
GreenMoney™ — Ages 12–18, finance & the green economy
4
SafeQuest™ — All ages, safety habits & compliance
👤

Creating a Profile

Most games support player profiles to track progress and achievements.

1
Click "Start Playing" on any game's landing page
2
Enter a display name and choose an avatar
3
For school accounts, use your organisation access code
4
Parent/guardian email recommended for players under 13
🔊

Audio & Narration

Several games feature AI narrators and voice guidance.

1
Allow browser audio permissions when prompted
2
Toggle voice on/off using the speaker icon in-game
3
All content is available in text — audio is never required
4
Headphones are recommended in classroom settings

🎮 Gameplay Guides

In-depth guides for each game's mechanics, strategy, and learning objectives.

All games are designed to be intuitive from the first session, but these step-by-step guides help you get the most out of every play.

🌱 EcoQuest™ — Environmental Action Game

A cooperative card game where all players win or lose together against environmental challenges.
1
Choose a mission: Clean the River, Restore Coral Reef, Plant the Forest, and 7+ more
2
Each player draws 3 action cards at the start of their turn
3
Match action types to mission requirements to earn eco-points
4
Complete the mission before the pollution deck runs out to win
5
Celebrate — a science fact about the ecosystem is revealed on completion
Play EcoQuest™ →

🧠 MindQuest™ — Inner World Adventure

A solo or cooperative journey through five Emotion Kingdoms using science-backed coping strategies.
1
Select your Hero — each has a unique emotional strength and starting ability
2
Draw Mission, Challenge, and Coping Tool cards each turn
3
Navigate emotion kingdoms (Emberland, Tidepools, Sunspire, Shadowmere, Stillwood)
4
Luna the narrator explains the real neuroscience behind each coping tool
5
Balance Hearts (HP) across all five kingdoms to complete the adventure
Play MindQuest™ →

💹 GreenMoney™ — Sustainable Finance Simulator

A turn-based market simulation where each player manages a green investment portfolio.
1
Choose your role: Green Investor, Carbon Trader, or ESG Analyst
2
Monitor the simulated market and respond to economic events each round
3
Buy/sell green stocks, renewable bonds, and carbon credits
4
Balance your Profitability Score with your Planet Impact Score
5
Navigate random events: climate disasters, policy changes, tech breakthroughs
Play GreenMoney™ →

🛡️ SafeQuest™ — Safety Habit Builder

A habit-building platform with zones, challenges, and a coin-based reward economy.
1
Complete your profile and the safety onboarding tutorial
2
Explore Safety Zones and complete daily habit challenges
3
Earn SafeCoins for completed challenges and streak bonuses
4
Spend coins in the Reward Marketplace for badges and recognition
5
Climb the leaderboard and unlock advanced safety audit challenges
Play SafeQuest™ →

👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents

Bodha™ games are designed to be played safely by children aged 7 and above. Here's how to support your child's learning journey.

🔒

Privacy & Child Safety

We take children's data privacy seriously. Bodha™ is fully COPPA and GDPR-K compliant.

1
No personal data beyond display name and age bracket is required
2
No in-game purchases — all content is accessible without payment
3
No social chat features for under-13 players
4
Request full data deletion anytime: privacy@bodha.in
⏰

Screen Time Guidance

Games are designed for focused 20–45 minute sessions, not endless loops.

1
EcoQuest / MindQuest: 20–30 min per session, 3–5 sessions recommended
2
GreenMoney: 30–45 min per full game round
3
SafeQuest daily check-ins: under 5 minutes
4
All games have natural stopping points — no artificial cliffhangers
💬

Conversation Starters

The best learning happens when play leads to real-world conversation:

1
"What eco mission did you complete? What did you learn?"
2
"Which emotion kingdom was hardest? Why do you think that is?"
3
"What green investment did you make? Would you do it differently?"
4
"What safety habit are you working on this week?"
📊

Tracking Progress

Parents with accounts can view their child's learning progress across all games.

1
Create a parent account and link it to your child's profile
2
View missions completed, concepts encountered, and time played
3
Download a learning report to share with your child's school
4
Set weekly learning goals and receive milestone notifications
