Bodha™ was born from a simple conviction: the most urgent problems facing our planet can only be solved if the next generation understands them deeply enough to act. We build the games that make that understanding possible.

Our Origin

Why we built Bodha

The word Bodha (बोध) is Sanskrit for awakening — the moment when understanding shifts from knowing facts to genuinely seeing the world differently. That is the experience we design every game to create.

We saw children who cared deeply about the planet but had no real tools to understand it. We saw teachers who wanted to teach sustainability but lacked engaging materials. We saw corporations struggling to build safety cultures that actually changed behaviour.

Every Bodha game is our answer to a specific, real problem — grounded in science, designed by educators, and crafted by game designers who know that the best learning happens when you forget you are learning at all.

"The most powerful thing a teacher can say is: let's play."
Game-based learning activates the same neural pathways as real experience. When a child saves a coral reef in EcoQuest, their brain does not know it is not real — and that is precisely the point. The emotion, the decision, the consequence — all of it encodes as memory.

What We Stand For

Our values

Six principles that guide every design decision, partnership, and line of code.

🌍

Planet First

Every game we build is anchored to at least one UN Sustainable Development Goal. Environmental responsibility is not a feature — it is the foundation.

🔬

Science Grounded

We never sacrifice accuracy for engagement. Every fact, statistic, and learning mechanic in our games is reviewed by subject-matter experts before it reaches a player.

🧒

Child-Centred Design

Children deserve real knowledge, not watered-down facts. We design games that respect children's intelligence and challenge them appropriately for their stage of development.

♾️

Relentlessly Scalable

Every technical and pedagogical decision is made with scale in mind. A platform that can reach one school can reach a million — if it is built right from the beginning.

🤝

Radically Accessible

Sustainability education cannot be only for those who can afford premium ed-tech. We price for the world we want to see, with free access for individuals and affordable tiers for schools.

📏

Measured Impact

We believe every educational intervention should be measured. Our platform tracks learning outcomes, not just engagement metrics — because what matters is what students actually learn.
